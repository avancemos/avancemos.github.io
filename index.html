Who Are We?

We are a group of aspiring young high school student nerds, techies, and geeks working to make it easier for online services to remain available to the public. We understand that there is no such thing as a free lunch and that online services are susidized by advertisements. Ad-blockers and the younger generation have reduced the value of web ads. Our solution to increase the value of these ads is to embed them directly into content.

Why use Avancemos?

At Your Service

Protect Your Ads From Ad-Blockers

Allows advertisers and platforms to circumvent ad-blockers by embedding ads directly in content.

Increase User Engagement

Increases user engagement with embedded ads and branding so that when users seek out content they automatically see the ads.

Demonstrate Your Brand's Popularity

Illustrates how popular your product is by showing how many people use your product.

Collect Market Data & Plan Ad Campaigns

Allows advertisers to collect market data easily and plan effective ad campaigns.

Application to Company like Snapchat

Images taken in Snapchat are uploaded to the Avancemos servers where they are modifeid to contain advertisements for our corporate sponsors. Avancemos-modified images are then sent to their recipients.
